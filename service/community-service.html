Community Service

Undergraduate Research - Annual Events

I have supported Undergraduate Research initiatives throughout my academic career, specifically underrepresented disciplines in research. Here are several community events I have supported over the years as an organizer, moderator, judge, or participant.

College of Business Administration RSCA Showcase

Cal Poly Pomona
Research Presentations, Undergraduate Research, Graduate Research, Business and Entrepreneurship

The College of Business Administration Research Scholarship and Creative Activities (RSCA) Showcase is held each spring to highlight student research, and creative activities from tied to the College of Business Administration at Cal Poly Pomona.

Student RSCA Conference (CPP-RSCA)

Cal Poly Pomona
Undergraduate Research, Graduate Research, Competition

The Student Research, Scholarship, and Creative Activities Conference is an annual event held in the spring for both undergraduate and graduate students from all disciplines to disseminate their work. This conference has both competitive and non-competitive sessions.

Read more!
Creative Activities & Research Symposium (CPP-CARS)

Cal Poly Pomona
Undergraduate Research, Graduate Research, Summer Research Programs

The Creative Activities & Research Symposium is an annual event held in the summer for both undergraduate and graduate students from all disciplines to disseminate their work. Summer research programs and REUs are encouraged to present!
